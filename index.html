Aerovyn

Faster Tuesdays, faster Sundays.

Aerovyn was founded in 2022 in Flagstaff, Arizona, by a small team of road and track coaches. The brand exists to fill the gap between a daily trainer and a carbon-plated racer - the workout shoe a competitive runner uses on Tuesday and Thursday. ThresholdFoam is a moderately firm midsole tuned for repeat efforts at half-marathon and 10K race pace, with a five-channel outsole pattern that flexes predictably at the metatarsal heads. The line is two workout shoes and one short-distance racer.
