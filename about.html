About Aerovyn

Aerovyn began as a coaching project. In 2021, three track and cross-country coaches based in Flagstaff — Sam Okafor, Hannah Lindgren, and Mateo del Río — were frustrated that their athletes were either training in shoes designed for easy miles or grinding their carbon-plated race shoes on workout days. The hard surface of a track and the repeat-effort nature of a threshold session called for something in between, and nothing on the market quite fit. They prototyped the first ThresholdFoam midsole in a small workshop near the NAU campus in late 2021 and launched the brand in spring 2022. The team is still small — under fifteen people as of 2026 — and most production decisions still go through Sam, Hannah, or Mateo.

Aerovyn is based in Flagstaff, Arizona and ships direct.
